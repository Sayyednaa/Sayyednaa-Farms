The Heritage

Our Story

Sayyednaa Farms is built on a foundation of tradition, integrity, and innovation. What started as a passion for pure, organic living has grown into a modern agricultural enterprise dedicated to providing the highest quality livestock and crops.

FOUNDER VETERINARY SPECIALIST

Sayyed Nawab Abdul Adil

With over 12 years of hands-on experience in Veterinary Science and Farm Management, Sayyed Nawab Abdul Adil is the guiding force behind the health and sustainability of Sayyednaa Farms. His deep expertise in animal welfare and organic breeding ensures that every aspect of our livestock operations meets the highest global standards of purity and care.

CO-FOUNDER SOFTWARE ENGINEER

Sayyed Nawab Abdul Ali

Bridging the gap between software engineering and agriculture, Sayyed Nawab Abdul Ali leads the technological evolution of our farm. From precision inventory tracking to data-driven crop management systems, his vision is to empower modern agriculture with digital intelligence, ensuring Sayyednaa Farms remains at the cutting edge of efficiency and sustainability.

Our Vision

"To redefine organic farming by combining ancestral wisdom with modern scientific veterinary care and digital precision, creating a transparent ecosystem of pure, wholesome food for the next generation."
