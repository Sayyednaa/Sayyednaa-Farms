Farmhouse Collection Only

Important Notice

To ensure the absolute freshness and quality of our organic produce and livestock, we do not offer shipping services. All items must be collected directly from Sayyednaa Farms.

1. Priority for Online Bookings

Customers who book their produce or livestock online will be given first priority. Due to limited daily harvest and high demand, we recommend booking in advance to ensure availability upon your arrival.

2. Collection Process

  • Place your order through our Farm Shop.
  • You will receive a confirmation message with your Order ID.
  • Visit the farmhouse during our operating hours (Mon-Sat: 8 AM - 6 PM).
  • Present your Order ID at the collection counter.

3. Why Collection Only?

By eliminating long-distance shipping, we reduce our carbon footprint and guarantee that your food hasn't spent days in a delivery truck. You get it straight from the source, exactly how nature intended.
